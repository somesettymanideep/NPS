Automatic Mains Failure (AMF) Panel

Our Automatic Mains Failure (AMF) Panel, also known as an Automatic Transfer Switch, is engineered to provide seamless and reliable power management. The AMF Panel is an essential component in automatic generator installations, designed to automatically switch the power supply from the main grid to a diesel generator (DG) in the event of a mains failure, and switch back once normal supply resumes. This automation ensures uninterrupted operation for systems where continuous power is critical.

We offer a full range of AMF solutions, including Manual, Semi-Automatic, and Fully Automatic options, all available with a microprocessor-based controller. These controllers are highly customizable, allowing us to configure each panel to meet specific client requirements.
