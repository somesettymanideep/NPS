About Company

Your Partner in Electrifying Excellence

Nissi Power Solutions is a premier manufacturer and supplier in the electrical industry, specializing in high-quality electrical panel boards and cable trays tailored for commercial, industrial, and domestic applications. Established in 2005 and headquartered in Vijayawada, we have built a solid reputation for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. At Nissi Power Solutions, we believe in delivering "Industrial Solutions Empowered by Us.
